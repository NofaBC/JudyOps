How JudyOps Works

Four simple steps to turn one repetitive business process into a guided AI workflow.

1

Choose the Workflow

We start with one repetitive task or process that takes too much time — such as following up with new leads, answering the same customer questions, collecting appointment information, routing requests, gathering documents, or preparing summaries.

2

Build the Workflow Logic

We map what JudyOps should ask, answer, collect, recommend, and escalate — including approved answers, required questions, forms and links, contact information, routing rules, escalation rules, and the summary sent to the owner.

3

Launch JudyOps

JudyOps is added where the workflow begins — on a website, QR code, landing page, product page, intake form, or internal process page. Once live, JudyOps guides people through the workflow instead of only answering basic questions.

4

Review, Improve, and Expand

The business owner receives summaries, insights, and recommendations. Over time, JudyOps can improve the workflow and expand into additional business processes. Start with one workflow. Add more as the business sees results.
